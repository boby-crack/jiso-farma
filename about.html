ABOUT US

PT Gunsas Jaya Berkah is a company dedicated to promoting sustainability and well-being in the health sector. Our commitment to economic, environmental, social, and governance (ESG) aspects is deeply ingrained in all our activities, as we strive to contribute to the overall health of the nation.

As we navigate the ever-evolving landscape of the healthcare industry, PT Gunsas Jaya Berkah is determined to play an active role in improving the health and lives of Indonesians. With a focus on sustainability and a holistic approach to healthcare, we are driven to deliver innovative solutions and foster a healthier society for generations to come.

Join us on this journey towards a healthier and more sustainable future, where the well-being of individuals and the progress of our nation go hand in hand. Together, let us build a brighter tomorrow through PT Gunsas Jaya Berkah.

VISION

To be the best choice of locally produced vaccine products for all elements of the nation.

MISSION

Develop and produce various types of vaccines by prioritizing quality and continuous improvement with the sustainable application of technological advances for the welfare of all stakeholders.
